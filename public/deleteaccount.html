Requesting Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data from No Cash Tipper, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your No Cash Tipper account using your credentials.
  2. Navigate to your Links page.
  3. Find and long press on the "Delete My Account" or similar option.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request.
  5. Your account and associated data will be scheduled for deletion.

Please note that the deletion process may take some time.

Data Deletion and Retention

When you request the deletion of your account, we will delete the following types of data associated with your account:

  • Your personal information, including your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Any data associated with your account in our databases.

We may retain some data for a limited period, including:

  • Information required for legal or compliance purposes.
  • Data necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.
  • Aggregated and anonymized data used for analytics and improving our services.
